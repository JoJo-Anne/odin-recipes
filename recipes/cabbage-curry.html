Cabbage Curry

Description

Cabbage Curry is a delicious mildly spiced vegetarion Indian dish made with cabbage, peas and aromatic spices.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seads
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon coriander
  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • 1 onion thinley sliced
  • 1/2 cabbage thinley sliced
  • 1 cup of frozen peas
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Fry the mustard and cumin seads in a Wok with the oil until they start to pop
  2. Add the ginger and stir for about a minute until fragrant before adding the onion.
  3. Add spices and fry for a minute or 2 to remove the raw taste before adding the cabbage.
  4. Add the frozen peas and salt and stir well.
  5. Cook until the cabbage is at your desired tenderness.
